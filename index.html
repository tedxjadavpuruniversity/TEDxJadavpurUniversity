Jadavpur University has been organising TEDX since 2017 with the first TEDx theme as "Spectrum" celebrating diversity and bringing together speakers from diverse backgrounds. This March it will be the third TEDx event. Organisers are students of Jadavpur University interested in spreading ideas worth knowing.

The chosen theme this 2019 is "Reimagining the Future". We are at an intriguing point in history where situations and circumstances need to evolve with the changing times.

TEDxJadavpurUniversity would like to create a space for a point of dialogue to arise between future leaders and visionaries and the present game changers who will lead them to view and build the future in an inclusive, sustainable and innovative way. We think it is quite important to understand each other and allow other to voice their perspectives and in doing so create something long term and inclusive.

Everyone will have a different perspective when imagining the future. TEDxJadavpurUniversity brings together voices and observations from a diverse background and various fields in an effort of reimagining the future.

Last year at TEDxJadavpurUniversity we had brilliant and inspiring speakers talking about breaking the barriers in our own minds and in society. And it is now time to take last year's experiences and create something holistic and inclusive out of it all. The future promises to challenge and break our belief systems.

Join us on the 28th March at Jadavpur University to be part of something enriching.
